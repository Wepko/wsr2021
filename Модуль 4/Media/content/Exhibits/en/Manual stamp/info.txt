Short name: Manual stamp
Name: Manual stamp
Year: 1980
Description: Stamp, hand made, wooden. In the pre-stamping period, stamps performed two main functions: they were a sign of postage and a check mark for the delivery of mail. When postage stamps began to perform the function of a payment sign, postage stamps began to be used to cancel them.
